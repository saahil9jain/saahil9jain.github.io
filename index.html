Saahil Jain

I am currently working on You.com, a new search engine that summarizes the web for you.

At Stanford, I researched methods to develop AI in resource-constrained domains such as healthcare under Professor Andrew Ng in the Stanford Machine Learning Group. I'm broadly excited by artificial intelligence (AI), deep learning, and natural language processing (NLP). Much of my research focuses on developing better methods of learning from limited/noisy labels and building multimodal datasets/models.

I’ve worn various hats so far, working across engineering, product, and research. I received my MS in Computer Science at Stanford University. Prior to Stanford, I worked as a product manager at Microsoft for Outlook within Office 365. I received my BS at Columbia University, where I studied Computer Science and Economics.

To get in touch, feel free to email me at saahil.jain@cs.stanford.edu.
